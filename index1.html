Welcome to Genshin Impact

This is a web application for Genshin Impact.

Context

Genshin Impact is a mobile game developed by miHoYo. It is a role-playing game that takes place in the world of Mondstadt, a city in the northeastern region of Liyue.

Features

  • Character selection: Choose from a wide range of characters, including characters from different rarities and professions.
  • Battle system: Fight battles with your characters to earn rewards and experience points.
  • Shop: Purchase items from the in-game store to enhance your character's abilities and equipment.
  • Quests: Complete quests to earn rewards and experience points.

Download

Genshin Impact is available on both iOS and Android platforms. You can download the game from the official website or through the app stores.
